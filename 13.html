definitions

pick an arbitrary point

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$p_0 \in \mathbb{C} \text{neu} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

point on a line or circle (inputs a line or a circle outputs a point)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$p_0 \in L_1 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

line across two points \(a_0\) and \(a_1\) (inputs two points outputs a line)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$L\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_0 , a_1} = L\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_1 , a_0} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

circle at a center \(a_0\) across \(a_1\) (inputs two points outputs a circle)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_0 , a_1} \neq C\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_1 , a_0} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

point intersection between two lines \(L_1\) and \(L_2\) (inputs two lines outputs a point)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$L_1 \cap L_2 = L_2 \cap L_1 = \{a\} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

point intersections between two circles \(C_1\) and \(C_2\) (inputs two circles outputs max two points)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C_1 \cap C_2 = C_2 \cap C_1 = \{a_1 , a_2\} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

point intersections between a line and a circle \(L_1\) and \(C_1\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$L_1 \cap C_1 = C_1 \cap L_1 = \{a_1 , a_2\} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

circle at a center \(a_0\) with radius from \(a_1\) to \(a_2\) (a compass, inputs three points outputs a circle)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C\overline{\phantom{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_0 , a_1 , a_2} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

Parallel Line | line through \(a_1\) parallel to \(L_0\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$a_0 \in : L_0 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$L_1 \ni : L\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_0 , a_1} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$r \in : \mathbb{C} \text{neu} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C_1 \ni : C\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_0 , r} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C_2 \ni : C\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{|}} a_1 , a_0 , r} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$a_2, a_3 \in : L_0 \cap C_1 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$a_4, a_5 \in : L_1 \cap C_1 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$a_6, a_7 \in : L_1 \cap C_2 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$C_4 \ni : C\overline{\phantom{\overset{0}{0}}a_6 , a_4 , a_2} \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$a_8, a_9 \in : C_2 \cap C_4 \\$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

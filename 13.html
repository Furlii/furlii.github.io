point intersections of two circles

$$\begin{array}{llll} zz^* &-z a_0^* &-a_0 z^* &= a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* \\ \\ zz^* &-z b_0^* &-b_0 z^* &= b_1 b_1^* -b_1 b_0^* -b_0 b_1^* \\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

isolate \(z^*\)

$$zz^* -a_0 z ^* -z {a_0 }^* = a_1 {a_1 }^* -a_1 {a_0 }^* -a_0 {a_1 }^*$$

$$z^* (z -a_0) -z {a_0 }^* = a_1 {a_1 }^* -a_1 {a_0 }^* -a_0 {a_1 }^*$$

$$z^* (z -a_0) = a_1 a_1 ^* -a_1 a_0 ^* -a_0 a_1 ^* -z a_0 ^*$$

$$z^* = \dfrac{a_1 a_1 ^* -a_1 a_0 ^* -a_0 a_1 ^* -z a_0 ^*}{z -a_0}$$

$$zz^* -z b_0 ^* -b_0 z ^* = b_1 b_1 ^* -b_1 b_0 ^* -b_0 b_1 ^*$$

$$z\left(\dfrac{\dots}{z-a_0}\right) -z b_0^* -b_0 \left(\dfrac{\dots}{z-a_0}\right) = b_1 b_1^* - b_1 b_0^* - b_0 b_1^*$$

$$z\left(\dfrac{\dots}{z-a_0}\right) -z b_0^* -b_0 \left(\dfrac{\dots}{z-a_0}\right) - b_1 b_1^* + b_1 b_0^* + b_0 b_1^*= 0$$

$$z\left(\dots\right) -z b_0 ^*(z-a_0) -b_0 \left(\dots\right) +\left(-b_1 b_1^* + b_1 b_0^* + b_0 b_1^*\right)(z-a_0)= 0$$

$$\begin{array}{lllll} +z(\dots) &= a_0 ^* z^2 &+z\Big( a_1 a_1 ^* -a_1 a_0 ^* -a_0 a_1 ^* \Big) \\ \\ -{b_0 }^*z(z-a_0) &= -b_0 ^* z^2 &+z( a_0 b_0 ^* ) \\ \\ -b_0(\dots) &= &+z(-a_0 ^* b_0 ) &-b\Big( a_1 a_1 ^* -a_1 a_0 ^* -a_0 a_1 ^* \Big) \\ \\ (\dots)(z-a_0) &= &-z\Big( b_1 b_1 ^* -b_1 b_0 ^* -b_0 b_1 ^* \Big) &+a_0 \Big( b_1 b_1 ^* -b_1 b_0 ^* -b_0 b_1 ^* \Big) \\ \\ \end{array}$$

point intersections of two circles (3rd attempt)

recall

$$\begin{array}{llll} zz^* &-z a_0^* &-a_0 z^* &= a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* \\ \\ zz^* &-z b_0^* &-b_0 z^* &= b_1 b_1^* -b_1 b_0^* -b_0 b_1^* \\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

are eqns of circles at \(a_0\) through \(a_1\) and at \(b_0\) through \(b_1\) respectively

the sets of points \(a_0 , a_1 , b_0 , b_1\)

$$\{a_0 , a_1 , b_0 , b_1\}$$

translate \(a_0\) to point of origin\(0+0i\)

$$\{0 , a_1 -a_0 , b_0 -a_0 , b_1 -a_0\}$$

multiply set such that \(b_0 -a_0\) become \(1+0i\)

$$\left\{0 , \dfrac{a_1 -a_0}{b_0 -a_0} , 1 , \dfrac{b_1 -a_0}{b_0 -a_0}\right\}$$

let $$\begin{array}{rrrr} f_0 &= \dfrac{a_1 -a_0}{b_0 -a_0}\\ \\ f_1 &= \dfrac{b_1 -a_0}{b_0 -a_0}\\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

so $$\{0 , f_0 , 1 , f_1\}$$

the equations are now

$$\begin{array}{llll} zz^* &-z (0)^* &-(0) z^* &= f_0 f_0^* -f_0 (0)^* -(0) f_0^* \\ \\ zz^* &-z (1)^* &-(1) z^* &= f_1 f_1^* -f_1 (1)^* -(1) f_1^* \\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

$$\begin{array}{llll} zz^* & & &= f_0 f_0^* \\ \\ zz^* &-z &- z^* &= f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^* \\ \\ z^* & & &= \dfrac{f_0 f_0^*}{z}\\ \\ f_0 f_0^* &-z &- \dfrac{f_0 f_0^*}{z} &= f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^* \\ \\ f_0 f_0^* z &-z^2 &- f_0 f_0^* &= (f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^*)z \\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

results in a quadratic

$$f^2 + (f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^* -f_0 f_0^*)f - f_0 f_0^* = 0$$

solving this quadratic gives

$$2f = -(f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^* -f_0 f_0^*) \pm \sqrt{\Big( f_1 f_1^* \Big)^2 -2\Big( f_1 f_1^* \Big)\Big(f_1 +f_1^* +f_0 f_0^*\Big) +\Big(f_1 +f_1^* +f_0 f_0^*\Big)^2}$$

placing it back into its original position and orientation

$$\dfrac{z-a_0}{b_0-a_0} = f$$

$$z = f(b_0-a_0) + a_0$$

gives

$$2z = 2f(b_0-a_0) + 2a_0$$

$$2z = 2a_0 -(f_1 f_1^* -f_1 -f_1^* -f_0 f_0^*)(b_0-a_0) \pm (b_0-a_0)\sqrt{\Big( f_1 f_1^* \Big)^2 -2\Big( f_1 f_1^* \Big)\Big(f_1 +f_1^* +f_0 f_0^*\Big) +\Big(f_1 +f_1^* +f_0 f_0^*\Big)^2}$$

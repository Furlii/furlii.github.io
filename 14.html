definitions

types of coordinates that will be used

$$\phantom{|}$$

cartesian 2d

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$(x, y) \text{ or } \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

cartesian 3d

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$(x, y, z) \text{ or } \begin{pmatrix} x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

and \(\mathbb{C}\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$x+iy$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

Projection | displaying 3D in a 2D

$$\phantom{|}$$

with \( (0, 0) \) as the center of both coordinate and \(d\) as depth or distance (to the camera)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$f(x, y, z)= \left(\dfrac{x\cdot d}{z+d}, \dfrac{y \cdot d}{z+d}\right)$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

this uses the z axis for the "depth" axis

$$\phantom{|}$$

meanwhile if we use y as depth axis and z for height axis

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$f(x, y, z)= \left(\dfrac{x\cdot d}{y+d}, \dfrac{z \cdot d}{y+d}\right)$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

in this case the camera will be located at \( \Big(0, -d, 0\Big) \) facing towards positive \(y\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

with \(v_x , v_y\) as vanishing point

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$f(x, y, z)= \left(v_x+\dfrac{(x-v_x)\cdot d}{y+d}, v_y+\dfrac{(z-v_y) \cdot d}{y+d}\right)$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

Translation | by \((a_x\quad,\quad a_y\quad,\quad a_z)\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$f\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}+\begin{pmatrix}a_x \\ a_y \\ a_z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$x\rightarrow x+a_x$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$y\rightarrow x+a_y$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$z\rightarrow x+a_z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

Rotation | by \(\theta\)

$$\phantom{|}$$

over \(x\) axis , \(y\) axis , and \(z\) axis

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$Rx\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}1& 0& 0 \\ 0& \cos\theta& -\sin\theta \\ 0& \sin\theta& \cos\theta\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$x\rightarrow x$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$y\rightarrow \cos\theta y -\sin\theta z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$z\rightarrow \sin\theta y +\cos\theta z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$Ry\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta& 0& \sin\theta \\ 0&1&0 \\ -\sin\theta&0& \cos\theta\end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$x\rightarrow \cos\theta x +\sin\theta z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$y\rightarrow y$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$z\rightarrow -\sin\theta x +\cos\theta z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$Rz\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix} \cos\theta& -\sin\theta& 0 \\ \sin\theta & \cos\theta & 0 \\ 0&0&1 \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$x\rightarrow \cos\theta x -\sin\theta y$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$y\rightarrow \sin\theta x +\cos\theta y$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$z\rightarrow z$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

Homothety | by scaling factor of \(k\)

$$k\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix} = \begin{pmatrix}k&0&0 \\ 0&k&0 \\ 0&0&k \end{pmatrix}\begin{pmatrix}x \\ y \\ z\end{pmatrix}$$

$$x\rightarrow kx$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$y\rightarrow ky$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

$$z\rightarrow kz$$

$$\phantom{|}$$

line across two points \(a_0\) and \(a_1\)

$$(z-a_0)(a_1-a_0)^*=(z-a_0)^*(a_1-a_0) \\$$

$$z(a_1-a_0)^*-a_0(a_1-a_0)^*=z^*(a_1-a_0)-a_0^*(a_1-a_0) \\$$

$$z(a_1-a_0)^*-a_0a_1^*+a_0a_0^*=z^*(a_1-a_0)-a_0^*a_1+a_0^*a_0 \\$$

$$z(a_1-a_0)^*-a_0a_1^*=z^*(a_1-a_0)-a_0^*a_1 \\$$

$$z(a_1-a_0)^*-z^*(a_1-a_0)=a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1 \\$$

circle at a center point across a point

$$(z-a_0)(z-a_0)^*=(a_1-a_0)(a_1-a_0)^*$$

$$z(z-a_0)^*-a_0(z-a_0)^*=a_1(a_1-a_0)^*-a_0(a_1-a_0)^*$$

$$zz^*-z a_0^*-a_0z^*+a_0 a_0^*=a_1 a_1^*-a_1 a_0^*-a_0 a_1^* +a_0 a_0^*$$

$$zz^*-z a_0^*-a_0z^*=a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^*$$

point intersection between two lines

$$\begin{array}{llll} z(a_1-a_0)^*&-z^*(a_1-a_0)&=a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1\\ \\ z(b_1-b_0)^*&-z^*(b_1-b_0)&=b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1\\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

$$\begin{array}{llll} z(a_1-a_0)^*(b_1-b_0) &-z^*(a_1-a_0)(b_1-b_0) &= (a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1)(b_1-b_0)\\ \\ z(b_1-b_0)^*(a_1-a_0) &-z^*(b_1-b_0)(a_1-a_0) &= (b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1)(a_1-a_0)\\ \hline \\ z\Big((a_1-a_0)^*(b_1-b_0)-(b_1-b_0)^*(a_1-a_0)\Big)&&= (a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1)(b_1-b_0)-(b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1)(a_1-a_0)\\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

$$z = \dfrac{(a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1)(b_1-b_0)-(b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1)(a_1-a_0)}{(a_1-a_0)^*(b_1-b_0)-(b_1-b_0)^*(a_1-a_0)}$$

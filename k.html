point intersection between two lines

$$\begin{array}{llll} z(a_1-a_0)^*&-z^*(a_1-a_0)&=a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1\\ \\ z(b_1-b_0)^*&-z^*(b_1-b_0)&=b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1\\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

one may chose to solve by linear algebra

$$\begin{array}{llll} z(a_1-a_0)^*(b_1-b_0) &-z^*(a_1-a_0)(b_1-b_0) &= (a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1)(b_1-b_0)\\ \\ z(b_1-b_0)^*(a_1-a_0) &-z^*(b_1-b_0)(a_1-a_0) &= (b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1)(a_1-a_0)\\ \hline \\ z\Big((a_1-a_0)^*(b_1-b_0)-(b_1-b_0)^*(a_1-a_0)\Big)&&= (a_0a_1^*-a_0^*a_1)(b_1-b_0)-(b_0b_1^*-b_0^*b_1)(a_1-a_0)\\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

point intersections of two circles

$$\begin{array}{llll} zz^* &-z a_0^* &-a_0 z^* &= a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* \\ \\ zz^* &-z b_0^* &-b_0 z^* &= b_1 b_1^* -b_1 b_0^* -b_0 b_1^* \\ \\ \\ \end{array}$$

isolate \(z^*\)

$$zz^* -a_0 z^* -z a_0^* = a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^*$$

$$z^* (z -a_0) -z a_0^* = a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^*$$

$$z^* (z -a_0) = a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*$$

$$z^* = \dfrac{a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*}{z -a_0}$$

$$zz^* -z b_0^* &-b_0 z^* &= b_1 b_1^* -b_1 b_0^* -b_0 b_1^*$$

$$z\dfrac{a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*}{z -a_0} - z b_0^* - b_0 \dfrac{a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*}{z -a_0} = b_1 b_1^* -b_1 b_0^* -b_0 b_1^*$$

$$z\dfrac{a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*}{z -a_0} - z b_0^* - b_0 \dfrac{a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*}{z -a_0} - b_1 b_1^* +b_1 b_0^* +b_0 b_1^* =0$$

$$z\Big(a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*\Big) - z b_0^* (z -a_0) - b_0 \Big(a_1 a_1^* -a_1 a_0^* -a_0 a_1^* -z a_0^*\Big) + (-b_1 b_1^* +b_1 b_0^* +b_0 b_1^*)(z -a_0) =0$$
